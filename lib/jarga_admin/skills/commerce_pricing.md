# SKILL: Commerce Pricing Strategy

## Pricing principles
- **Cost-plus**: cost × (1 + margin). Typical retail margins: 50–80% for goods, 70–90% for digital.
- **Competitive**: align with market ± 10%
- **Value-based**: price based on perceived value, not cost
- **Bundle pricing**: offer sets at 10–15% discount vs individual items

## When suggesting price changes
1. Show current price and margin (if cost data available)
2. Show competitor range (if asked)
3. Calculate impact: revenue_change = (new_price - old_price) × estimated_units
4. Always confirm before updating: "Shall I update the price from £X to £Y?"

## Bulk price updates
If updating > 10 products, show data_table preview first.
Require explicit confirmation: "Yes, update all 14 prices."

## Price formatting
Always display as £X,XXX.XX (GBP). Avoid showing raw floats.

## Margin calculation
margin_pct = (price - cost) / price × 100
gross_profit = revenue × (margin_pct / 100)
